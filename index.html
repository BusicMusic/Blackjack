Black Light Black Jack
Created by BusicMusic

Rules

  • Get as close to 21 as possible without going over
  • Aces are counted as 1
  • All Face Cards are counted as 10
  • You are dealt two cards at the start
  • You can draw as many cards as you want
  • If you go over 21 you lose
  • The player who is the closest to 21 without going over wins
